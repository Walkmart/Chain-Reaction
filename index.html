How to Play Word Trail

Word Trail is a word association game where you need to guess the word that comes next in the chain based on the previous word.

Example:

If the word is Sun, the next word might be Screen.

Use your logic and word knowledge to complete the chain!

How it works:

  • You have 6 attempts to guess each word.
  • If you guess wrong, the tile will shake.
  • If you guess right, the tiles will turn green, and you move to the next word.
  • You can ask for hints, but they will reduce your score.
  • Complete the word chain to win!
