How to Play Chain Words

"Compundle" is a fun and challenging word game where players build a chain of words that compound on one another. Each word you guess connects to the next, and the goal is to uncover the full "bundle" of words, all linked together in clever ways.

Example:

If the word is Sun, the next word might be Screen.

Use your logic and word knowledge to complete the chain!

How it works:

  • You have 6 attempts to guess each word.
  • If you guess wrong, the tile will shake and you will lose 10 points.
  • If you guess right, the tiles will turn green, and you move to the next word.
  • You can ask for hints, but they will reduce your score by 5 points.
  • Complete the word chain to win!
